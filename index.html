The incredible journey begins!

We believe that more and more people share our concerns about the choices we as consumers make. They have huge consequences on our bodies as well as the environment. Yet, we don’t always have the time to address those consequences in our busy everyday lives. So, we created TrySome.

At Trysome we've done the hard work for you. We've built a network of partners whose products are good for you and for the planet. Now all you have to do is pick the products you want to try. All for free. That way there's no stress, no pressure. Try them, tell us your thoughts, and we help our partners continue to improve them for everyone to enjoy, including Mother Nature.

We're seeking to engage and inspire you to join us on this journey to change the world by changing the way we consume. Now, go try some!
